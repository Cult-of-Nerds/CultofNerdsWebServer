Explosive/Misc Recipes

Thermite: 1 parts aluminum powder 3 parts iron oxide Molotov Cocktail: 1 part gasoline 1 part motor oil and a gasoline soaked rag in the bottle neck Mace Substitute: 3 parts alcohol 1 part iodized salt Napalm: · Pour some gas into an old bowl, or some kind of container. · Get some styrofoam and put it in the gas, until the gas won't eat anymore. You should have a sticky syrup. · Put it on the end of something (don't touch it!!). The unused stuff lasts a long time!
